# Product Taxonomy Plan

Date: 2026-06-11

This document records the agreed product catalog strategy for the Diang Fire official website.

## Core Principle

The product center should use two parallel browsing systems:

1. Product type taxonomy: helps customers find what they need.
2. Brand taxonomy: helps customers browse by manufacturer.

Each product should exist as one record only. The same product can appear under both its product type page and its brand page through filters, instead of duplicating data.

## Primary Product Categories

### Respiratory Protection

Subcategories:

- Disposable particulate respirators
- Half-mask respirators
- Full-facepiece respirators
- Powered air-purifying respirators
- SCBA and air supply systems
- Facepieces, cylinders, and accessories

### Eye and Face Protection

Subcategories:

- Safety glasses
- Safety goggles
- Face shields
- Welding and special protection

### Hearing Protection

Subcategories:

- Earplugs
- Earmuffs
- Electronic earmuffs
- Communication and noise-reducing headsets

### Fall Protection

Subcategories:

- Full body harnesses
- Lanyards
- Self-retracting lifelines
- Anchors and lifelines
- Rescue kits

### Gas and Flame Detection

Subcategories:

- Fixed gas detectors
- Portable gas detectors
- Combustible gas detection
- Toxic gas and oxygen detection
- Flame detectors

### Fire SCBA and Emergency Equipment

Subcategories:

- Positive pressure fire SCBA
- Fire respirator facepieces
- Spare cylinders
- Fire rescue support equipment

### Firefighter PPE

Subcategories:

- Firefighting suits
- Fire boots
- Fire gloves
- Fire helmets
- Technical rescue suits

This category can remain as a reserved category until verified products and images are available.

### Extinguishing and Emergency Lighting

Subcategories:

- Fire extinguishers
- Fire suppression systems
- Explosion-proof lighting
- Portable scene lighting
- Emergency signs

This category can remain as a reserved category until verified products and images are available.

## Brand Categories

Current brand entries:

- 3M
- Honeywell
- MSA

Brand should be stored as a required product field and used as a filter or brand landing page. Brand should not replace product type classification.

## Product Management Fields

Every product should keep these fields:

- Brand
- Primary product category
- Product subcategory
- Series
- SKU / model number
- Chinese product name
- English product name
- Product summary
- Specification and standards
- Source document or official source URL
- Main image
- Additional images
- Product status: draft, verified, published, archived

## Website Experience

The product center should support:

- Browse by product category.
- Browse by brand.
- Filter a category by brand.
- Filter a brand page by product type.
- Show comparable products from different brands in the same product category.

For example, a customer entering Respiratory Protection should be able to compare 3M disposable respirators, MSA half-mask respirators, and fire SCBA products under the same larger respiratory protection structure.

## Safety Rule

Product SKU, product name, standards, descriptions, and images must not be guessed. If a product image or source document is not fully verified, the product should remain draft or be marked as needing review before being promoted as a featured product.
